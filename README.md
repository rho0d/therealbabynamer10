# Privacy Policy for The Real Baby Namer

**Last updated:** December 12, 2025

## Overview

The Real Baby Namer (the "App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

## Information We Collect

**The Real Baby Namer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information.**

This includes, but is not limited to:

* Names entered into the app
* Personal information of any kind
* Usage data or analytics
* Device information
* Location data
* Cookies or tracking technologies

All functionality of the App runs locally on the user’s device.

## Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising services, or external APIs that collect or process user data.

## Children’s Privacy

The Real Baby Namer is suitable for all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, or from any user. Since no data is collected, no data is stored or shared.

## Data Security

Because the App does not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of personal data being accessed, misused, or disclosed.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If future versions of the App change how information is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and posted on this page.
